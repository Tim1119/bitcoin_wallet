    With Smart-Xchange you can

    Protect your identity

    With Smart-Xchange, there's no credit card number that malicious actors can collect in order to steal from you. In fact, it's even possible in some cases to send a payment without revealing your identity, almost like with physical money. You should, however, take note that some effort can be required to protect your privacy.

    Choose your own fee

    There is a very small fee using smart-Xchage, many wallets let you control how large a fee to pay when spending. Most wallets have reasonable default fees, and higher fees can encourage faster confirmation of your transactions. Fees are unrelated to the amount transferred, so it's possible to send 100,000 bitcoins for the same fee it costs to send 1 bitcoin.

    Security and control over your money

    We treat all your details with care.So long as you take the required steps to protect your wallet, we can give you control over your money and a strong level of protection against many types of fraud.
